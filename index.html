What is Odyssey?

Odyssey is a platform developed for the use by the high school students where they can get consultation from people who have studied or are studying at a university of interest to the future applicant. Most of the times, if you do not have connection to that particular university student, it's difficult and improper to ask many questions. Using our platform you can now do that!

Services

First-hand Information

When you get a mentor, you can ask any questions regarding the concerned university.

University Experience

You might be curious to know about the student life. Your mentor will be happy to share his thoughts with you.

Documents Check

Mentor is responsible to making sure that your documents are well prepared.

On Time Delivery

When you sent a question or a request, mentor will get back to you within 48 hours. Usually the answer comes much earlier.
